Privacy Policy - Snuggle Birthday Manager

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: December 2024
Version: 1.0

This Privacy Policy describes how Snuggle Birthday Manager ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Contact Information: Names, phone numbers, and email addresses from your device's contact list (only with your permission)
  • Birthday Data: Dates you add manually or import from contacts
  • Account Information: Google account details if you choose to sign in with Google
  • Profile Information: Your name and profile picture from your Google account

1.2 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, features used, and crash reports
  • Notification Data: Information about notification preferences and delivery

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Purpose: We use your information solely to provide birthday reminder services and improve your experience with our app.

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Display upcoming birthdays and send reminder notifications
  • Sync your data across devices (if you use cloud backup)
  • Provide personalized app experience
  • Maintain your account and preferences

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve features and performance
  • Debug technical issues and prevent crashes
  • Ensure app security and prevent unauthorized access

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite databases. This means your birthday information remains private and accessible even without an internet connection.

3.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync, your data is securely stored in Firebase Firestore with the following protections:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure transmission using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security audits and compliance monitoring

3.3 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Secure authentication through Google Sign-In
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Limited access to personal data
  • No storage of payment information

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4.1 Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate our app (e.g., Google Firebase for cloud services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with prior notice)

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Data Control

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable cloud sync and use the app locally only

5.2 Notification Control

  • Enable or disable push notifications
  • Customize notification timing and frequency
  • Choose which types of reminders to receive

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure that all international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and provide adequate safeguards for your personal information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Sending you a notification through the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@snuggle-app.com
  • Developer: Bruno Oliveira
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

Your Trust Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This policy reflects our dedication to keeping your personal information safe and secure.
