4.1 Where We Store

  • Local device: Temporary cache and settings
  • Google Play Services: Authentication data

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit: HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: AES-256
  • Secure authentication: OAuth 2.0 with Google
  • Restricted access: Authorized personnel only
  • Automated backups
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity

4.3 Data Retention

  • Active data: While the account is active
  • Deleted data: Removed within 30 days
